Selected Projects

Progressive Learning & System Identification

Online Deterministic Annealing (ODA)

  • Abstract: A general-purpose learning model designed to meet the needs of applications in which computational resources are limited, and robustness and interpretability are prioritized.
  • Details: An online prototype-based learning algorithm based on annealing optimization that is formulated as a recursive gradient-free stochastic approximation algorithm.
  • Keywords: Explainable AI, Adaptive Clustering, Progressive Classification, Progressive Function Approximation, Explainable Reinforcement Learning.
  • Links: (TAC22) (TNNLS22) (GitHub)

Online Switched System Identification

  • Abstract: Online identification scheme for discrete-time piece-wise affine models based on a system of adaptive algorithms running in two timescales.
  • Details: A stochastic approximation algorithm based on online deterministic annealing estimates the partition of the space that defines the switching signal, as well as the number of modes. At the same time, recursive identification running at a higher timescale updates the parameters of the local models.
  • Keywords: Switched System Identification, Piecewise-Affine Systems, Online Deterministic Annealing.
  • Links: (TBD)

Risk-Sensitive Reinforcement Learning with Exponential Criteria

  • Abstract: A risk-sensitive reinforcement learning approach for Policy Gradient and Actor-Critic methods based on exponential criteria.

Interaction Laws of Networked Systems

Learning Swarm Interaction Dynamics from Density Evolution

  • Abstract: Learning swarm interaction laws by observing its density evolution with iterative PDE-constrained optimization.
  • Details: Modeling the interaction function as a Green's function for appropriately defined linear operator, allows for the hydrodynamic mean-field limit partial integro-differential equations to be efficiently solved as a system of simple PDEs.
  • Keywords: Swarm Dynamics, Mean-field Games, Coordination Laws Inference.
  • Links: (TCNS22)

Progressive Graph Partitioning Based on Information Diffusion

  • Abstract: Adaptive graph partitioning based on the spectral information of the underlying graph Laplacian matrix approximated with a decentralized diffusion algorithm.
  • Details: By propagating an impulse through the graph via a diffusion equation, each node construct a local representation containing spectral information. Online Deterministic Annealing (ODA) is used online for adaptive spectral clustering. The approach is suitable for large graphs, requires minimal hyper-parameter tuning, and provides online control over the complexity-accuracy trade-off.
  • Keywords: Garph Partitioning, Community Detection, Decentralized Spectral Clustering.
  • Links: (CDC21)

Detection of Dynamically Changing Leaders in Complex Swarms

  • Abstract: Leader detection based on Granger causality and the Online Deterministic Annealing (ODA) algorithm.
  • Details: Coordination law inference based on a port-Hamiltonian representation of autonomous swarm with leadership.
  • Keywords: Swarm Leader Detection, Port-Hamiltonian Systems.
  • Links: (GameSec20)

Human-Robot Interaction & Collaboration

A Human-Robot Interface based on Surface EEG Sensors

  • Abstract: A Brain-Machine Interface decoding visual attention into montion in 3D space.
  • Details: Suitable for robot teleoperation or as part of a shared-control robotic manipulation scheme. No restrictions imposed on blinking, head movement, etc. Appropriate for upper-extremity amputees.
  • Keywords: Brain-Machine Interface, Human-Robot Interaction, EEG-based Control.
  • Links: (IROS20)

Safe robot navigation with spatial and temporal constraints

  • Abstract: Robot Navigation Under MITL Constraints Using Time-Dependent Vector Field Based Control.
  • Details: Translating rudimental MITL formulas to control problems in the state space. Automatic design of a real-time controller as a hybrid automaton based on appropriately defined time-dependent vector fields.
  • Keywords: Safe Robot Navigation, Temporal Logic, Prescribed Performance Control.
  • Links: (CDC19)

Human-Robot Collaboration based on Motion Intention

  • Abstract: Physical Human-Robot Collaboration (HRC) scheme with adaptive impedance control under unknown robot dynamics.
  • Details: Human intention estimation through prescribed performance adaptive estimation law. No explicit communication, use of position, velocity and force/torque feedback.
  • Keywords: Human-Robot Collaboration, Human Intention Estimation, Prescribed Performance Control.
  • Links: (ECC18)

Intelligent Transportation

Real-Time Priority-Based Cooperative Highway Merging for Heterogeneous Autonomous Traffic

  • Abstract: A novel cooperative highway merging method taking into account heterogeneous, in terms of physical properties and priority, autonomous vehicles.
  • Details: Quadratic programming optimization with safety guarantees operating in real-time.
  • Keywords: Highway Merging, Mixed Traffic, Heterogeneous Autonomous Traffic Control.
  • Links: (ITSC21 - Best Student Paper Award)

Recursive Neural Networks

Reconstructing Strange Attractor Dynamics with Spiking Recurrent Neural Networks

  • Abstract: Recurrent spiking neural network architecture for learning and reconstruction of a strange attractor with partially observable states.
  • Details: Low-dimensional approximation based on Taken's Delay Embedding Theorem. Application in a heart-shaped oscillator and the Lorenz attractor.
  • Keywords: Spiking Neural Networks, Recurrent Neural Networks, Strange Attractor Dynamics, Nengo Brain Maker.
  • Links: (nengo.ai)
