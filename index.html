About

I am currently a Postdoc at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, working with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson.

I received my Ph.D. in electrical and computer engineering from the University of Maryland, College Park, in 2021, working with Prof. John S. Baras . Prior to that, I got my Diploma from the National Technical University of Athens, Greece, where I worked at the Control Systems Lab (CSL) with Prof. Kostas Kyriakopoulos.


Research Interests

Control Theory, System Identification and Learning Theory, Optimization, Multi-Agent Systems, Robotics.
